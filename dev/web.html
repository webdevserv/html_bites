BLOG: "Photo restoration using AI + GIMP"
Learn how to restore photographs while keeping their original essence. AI sometimes adds spurious results like adding make-up etc. and in this exercise we aim to preserve the spirit of the original photograph.
  1. Remove the background of the original image online with my FREE background remover app.
  2. Import the image and create a new background you like using AI Stable Diffusion online. I used www.plaground.ai.
  3. Merge the two images with a graphic design application, I used GIMP.
  4. Import the new created photograph to Stable Diffusion online and pass at 95 or higher image strength.
  5. Use inpaint in the Stable Diffusion app to fix the edges of the photograph and edit if needed.
  6. You have created a beautiful restored photograph that keeps the essence of the individuals in the original photo.
